# Customer Journey Map
## Overview
The customer journey map for cross-pay-gate outlines the phases a customer goes through when considering and using the blockchain-based cross-border payment platform. The phases are: Aware, Consider, Try, Adopt, and Expand.

### Aware
* **Trigger Event**: High fees or slow transaction times with current cross-border payment methods
* **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about blockchain-based payment solutions, perceived complexity of new technology
* **User Emotions**: Frustration, curiosity
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content highlighting the benefits of blockchain-based payments, case studies of successful implementations
* **Metric**: Website traffic, social media engagement (e.g., 1,000 unique website visitors, 500 social media followers)

### Consider
* **Trigger Event**: Research into alternative cross-border payment solutions
* **Friction Points**: Comparing features and pricing of different solutions, concerns about security and regulatory compliance
* **User Emotions**: Interest, skepticism
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Detailed product comparisons, whitepapers on security and compliance, customer testimonials
* **Metric**: Number of product demos requested, whitepaper downloads (e.g., 50 demo requests, 200 whitepaper downloads)

### Try
* **Trigger Event**: Signing up for a free trial or pilot program
* **Friction Points**: Technical difficulties with integration, uncertainty about scalability
* **User Emotions**: Excitement, caution
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Dedicated support team, comprehensive documentation, success stories from pilot programs
* **Metric**: Number of trial accounts, pilot program participation rate (e.g., 20 trial accounts, 80% pilot program participation)

### Adopt
* **Trigger Event**: Decision to implement cross-pay-gate as the primary cross-border payment solution
* **Friction Points**: Integration challenges, training staff on new system
* **User Emotions**: Relief, anticipation
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized onboarding process, ongoing customer support, regular software updates
* **Metric**: Number of paid accounts, customer retention rate (e.g., 15 paid accounts, 90% customer retention)

### Expand
* **Trigger Event**: Increased usage of cross-pay-gate, exploration of additional features
* **Friction Points**: Limited customization options, desire for more advanced analytics
* **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, ambition
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Customization options, premium support services, regular feature updates
* **Metric**: Average revenue per user (ARPU), net promoter score (NPS) (e.g., $10,000 ARPU, 40 NPS)
